The rolling grasslands of Kenya teem with elephant, zebra, and giraffe—and their powerful predators are often not far behind. Learn about elephant conservation from a representative of the Kenya Wildlife Service; watch for hundreds of species of birds in Amboseli National Park; and discover the Maasai way of life.

On this thrilling safari adventure, set out on daily wildlife drives in the shadow of Mount Kilimanjaro, Africa’s highest mountain.
